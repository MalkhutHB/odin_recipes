Vegan Apple Pie

Description

This is a delicious apple pie recipe. Made with no animal products, the crust is very buttery and light. Serve right out of the oven, chilled, or warmed

Ingredients

Crust:

  • 2 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup vegan margarine (such as Earth Balance®)
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Filling

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup vegan margarine (such as Earth Balance®)
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup rice milk
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 7 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored, and sliced
